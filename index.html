Orpheus Type Poster

I completed this poster for my Typography class. The assignment was to select a typeface and produce a poster encapsulating the name of the creator of the typeface, the year that typeface was created, and any forms we found particularly interesting.

My design focuses on the f, t, and g. The f and t demonstrate the sharpness of of some letters while the g demonstrates an elegantly round form while also including a sharp edge towards the top.

Orpheus Type Poster Part II

I completed this poster as the second part of the poster above. The assignment was to experiment with tracking and italics with the same typeface as well as include a short bio of the creator.
