I completed these posters for my Typography class. The assignment was to select a typeface and produce a poster encapsulating the name of the creator of the typeface, the year that typeface was created, and any forms we found particularly interesting.

My design for the first poster focuses on the f, t, and g. The f and t demonstrate the sharpness of of some letters while the g demonstrates an elegantly round form while also including a sharp edge towards the top.

The assignment for the second poster (scroll down) was to experiment with tracking and italics with the same typeface as well as include a short bio of the creator.
